Huaodor Adjustable Cat Door Latch & Holder

Keep your cat safe and comfortable while keeping your home secure with the Huaodor Adjustable Cat Door Latch. This durable, metal stopper is designed to let your cat access the outside while preventing unwanted access from dogs, babies, or children. Its adjustable design ensures a perfect fit for most standard doors, and it installs quickly with no adhesive or cutting required.

The Huaodor cat door latch is a versatile solution for pet owners who want to protect their cat's personal space. The wide gap allows easy entry for your feline friend, while the narrower setting keeps larger pets like dogs out. It's also the ideal way to secure a cat's litter box room without compromising the flow of air or the safety of your little ones.

Installing the Huaodor cat door holder is simple. Just remove the original strike plate and replace it with the Huaodor version using the existing screws. This convenient setup ensures your door stays in a partially open position, offering both functionality and safety. Its lightweight yet strong construction means it won't bend or break, unlike cheap plastic alternatives.

Designed for easy one-handed operation, this pet door latch is perfect for busy households. It allows seamless entry and exit for your cat while keeping other pets and children out. The sleek, painted metal finish looks great in any home setting and maintains its appearance over time, adapting well to your lifestyle needs.

A smart, safe, and stylish choice for pet door security.
