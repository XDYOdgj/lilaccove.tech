TGIOW Cat Door Latch - Keep Pets Safe & Give Your Cat Freedom

Let your cat enjoy the freedom of moving between rooms without worrying about unwanted visitors. The TGIOW Cat Door Latch is a smart solution for pet owners who want to keep dogs out of the litter box and food area while allowing cats to enter and exit freely. This pet door safety device ensures peace of mind for both you and your furry friends.

Designed with 5 adjustable size holes, this cat door latch can be easily customized to fit a variety of cat sizes. The adjustable width ranges from 2.5 to 6 inches, making it versatile and user-friendly. No need to measure or cut the door, it installs in seconds and works seamlessly with your existing door frame.

Built to last, the TGIOW cat door strap is made from durable PE and stainless steel materials, ensuring it can withstand the activity of large dogs. This pet door holder latch is easy to open with just one hand, giving you simple access from inside or outside the room when needed.

Safety is our top priority. When not in use, the flexible cat door holder latch hangs neatly on the door handle, reducing the risk of tripping or injury. Ideal for homes with active children, this device keeps the space safe and tidy without any exposed hardware.

We stand by the quality of our products. The TGIOW cat door latch is patented (US Patent 11-795741-B1) and registered with the Transparency Program to ensure authenticity and prevent counterfeit versions. Scan the code on the product to verify that you're getting the genuine article.
