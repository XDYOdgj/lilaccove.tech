YEEKORO Non-Inflatable Back Seat Extender

A durable and comfortable solution for pets and passengers, ideal for travel and camping adventures.

Why Choose YEEKORO?

Our car back seat extender is designed to maximize the space in your vehicle, transforming the back seat into a safe, functional area. With a heavy-duty construction that can support up to 440 lbs, it's built for both pets and people. Whether you're on a road trip, camping, or just heading out for a short drive, this extender offers a versatile and reliable option.

Comfort Meets Safety

The extender features a premium PU leather surface with a 5mm cotton layer for a soft and comfortable feel. It's perfect for pets who want to rest during travel or for passengers looking for more room. The sturdy 3 PP hollow plates on the bottom provide extra structural support, ensuring that the extender remains flat and stable even under heavy use. With a safety barrier design, it helps keep everyone secure during sudden stops or emergency braking.

Easy to Use and Clean

Measuring 51x25 inches, this extender is suitable for a wide range of vehicles including cars, SUVs, and small trucks. It's easy to install and remove, and can be quickly cleaned using a damp cloth or vacuum. The included carrying bag makes it simple to transport and store, so you can take it with you on any adventure without hassle.

Thoughtful Design Features

Enhance your travel experience with the added convenience of a mesh window on the extender, offering better visibility and breathability. This is especially great for dogs who enjoy keeping an eye on their surroundings. The extender also includes two practical mesh storage pockets for holding snacks, water bottles, and personal items, keeping your belongings organized and accessible.

Waterproof and Anti-Slip Performance

Durable waterproof PU leather ensures that spills and dirt are kept away from the car interior, keeping everything clean and fresh. The anti-slip bottom made of 210D material provides stability, preventing shifting during long journeys, making it safe and secure for all users.

Dual-Use Flexibility

What sets YEEKORO apart is its unique design that allows for multi-functional use. When folded, it can serve as an additional seating area for children or even as a personal seat for the driver. This added versatility makes it a must-have accessory for anyone who values both comfort and practicality in their vehicle.

Experience the perfect blend of durability, comfort, and functionality with YEEKORO's back seat extender. Ideal for road trips, camping, and everyday travel, it's a great companion for pets and passengers alike.
