Fresh Step Cat Litter Crystals in Tropical Summer Breeze Scent

Keep your home smelling fresh and clean with this innovative cat litter solution designed to fight odors effectively while being gentle on your cat and your family.

Litter Box Odor Eliminator - Fresh & Clean Every Time

Fresh Step cat litter crystals are specially formulated to release a light, pleasant fragrance every time your cat uses the litter box. This helps to conceal unwanted smells and keep your litter pan and your home smelling fresh and inviting.

100% Non-Toxic - Safe for You and Your Pet

These crystals are safe, non-toxic, and pet-friendly. You can rest assured that your kitty is not exposed to harsh chemicals, making this litter a reliable choice for households with pets, especially cats.

No Residue - Effortless Clean-Up

Unlike traditional litters, Fresh Step crystals are water-soluble and disintegrate completely over time. This means no messy clumps, no residue, and a clean, easy-to-maintain litter box experience.

Activated Every Time - Constant Freshness

The crystals are activated every time your cat steps on them, providing continuous odor control and freshening your litter pan automatically. This means less work for you and a better experience for your pet.

Summer Breeze Scent - A Refreshing Choice

Tropical Summer Breeze delivers a calming, citrusy scent that brings a sense of freshness and serenity to your living space. This subtle aroma keeps your home feeling clean and pleasant, helping you forget you're even using cat litter.

Why Choose Fresh Step Cat Litter?

Fresh Step cat litter crystals are the perfect blend of effective odor control, safety, and convenience. Whether you're a cat owner looking to simplify your routine or someone who values a clean, fresh home, these crystals are designed to meet your needs with style and care.
