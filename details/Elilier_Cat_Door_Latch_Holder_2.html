Elilier Cat Door Latch Holder

Keep pets safe and your home organized with this innovative cat door solution. Designed to keep dogs out and let cats in, it's the perfect way to maintain privacy and separation in your living space.

Keep Dogs Out, Let Cats In

The Elilier cat door stopper features a unique design with pink cat paws that makes it easy for your feline friend to access the room while keeping your dog out of the litter box and food area. This ensures your cat can use the bathroom or eat in peace, without any unwanted interruptions.

Adjustable Sizes for Every Door

With 8 adjustable sizes, this product fits door gaps ranging from 1.5" to 8". The flexible design allows it to work with both flat and rounded door knobs, making it ideal for various home setups. As your pet grows, you can easily adjust the size to match their needs.

No Tools Needed, Easy to Install

Installation is simple and requires no tools or alterations to your walls or door. Simply hook the stopper around your door knob and secure it into the latch hole. It's easy to remove and reattach as needed, ensuring convenience and flexibility.

Durable and Versatile Material

Crafted from high-quality plastic, this latch holder is both durable and flexible, so it won't break and can easily conform to your door's shape. Its resilience makes it long-lasting, and the attachment becomes more secure with time.

Better Airflow and Safety

This cat door latch not only keeps the door open but also promotes better airflow, helping to reduce energy costs and improve indoor air quality. It allows for natural ventilation without letting the door slam or blow open, making it safer and more comfortable for your pet.

The Perfect Pet Door Solution

Whether you're looking to manage your pet's access, reduce clutter, or maintain privacy, the Elilier cat door latch is a reliable and user-friendly choice. It's designed with your cat's comfort and your peace of mind in mind, making it a must-have for pet owners.
