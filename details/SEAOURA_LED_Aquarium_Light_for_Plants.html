SEAOURA LED Aquarium Light for Plants - Full Spectrum Fish Tank Light

Enhance your aquatic environment with the SEAOURA full spectrum aquarium light. Designed to support plant growth and provide a natural, vibrant glow for your fish, this LED light integrates advanced features like timer control, adjustable brightness, and multiple color modes for a customizable experience.

Three Customizable Settings

Enjoy the flexibility of choosing from three lighting modes: time, brightness, and color. Set your timer for 6, 10, or 12 hours to suit your aquarium's needs. Adjust brightness through 10 levels for optimal lighting conditions. Choose from white, red, green, pink, or only blue modes to create the perfect atmosphere for your tank's ecosystem.

Full Spectrum Illumination

This aquarium light delivers a full spectrum, promoting healthy growth for aquatic plants and enhancing fish vitality. Its intuitive controller ensures ease of use, making it ideal for both beginners and experienced aquarists. Whether you're setting up a new tank or maintaining an existing one, this light offers consistent and reliable performance.

High-Quality LED with Excellent CRI

Equipped with premium SMD2835 LED chips, this aquarium light emits a soft, natural glow. With a color rendering index of up to 89%, it closely mimics sunlight, making your underwater world look more vivid and realistic. The 6500K white light combined with red, green, pink, and blue hues provides a comprehensive lighting solution for all aquatic environments.

Durable Design with Extendable Bracket

Featuring 5 columns of LED chips, this light is not only compact but also improves the aesthetics of your aquarium. The strong, durable metal bracket ensures stability while allowing for easy extension to fit most tank sizes. The ABS shell offers efficient heat dissipation, maintaining the longevity and performance of the light.

Perfect for 18-24 Inch Tanks

Ideal for aquariums ranging from 18 to 24 inches, this light is versatile enough to work with both glass and acrylic tanks. It comes with a memory function that retains your previous settings even after a power outage. The light gradually rises and decreases over 15 minutes, creating a gentle and soothing environment for your aquatic pets. Simply install it with ease, and enjoy the natural beauty it brings to your tank day after day.
