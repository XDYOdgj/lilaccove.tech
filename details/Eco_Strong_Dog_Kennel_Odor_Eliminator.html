Eco Strong Dog Kennel Odor Eliminator Concentrate

A 2-in-1 solution for pet owners who need a powerful cleaner and odor eliminator in one product. Safe for use around pets and people, this concentrated formula works on both hard and soft surfaces, indoors and outdoors.

Why Choose Eco Strong?

Eco Strong offers an all-in-one cleaning and odor removal formula that’s perfect for homes, kennels, and pet facilities. Its advanced formula combines natural degreasing agents with enzymes to tackle stubborn pet stains and eliminate persistent odors, making it ideal for everyday use.

Powerful Odor Elimination

This concentrated cleaner quickly neutralizes pet waste smells, including urine, feces, and other organic messes. The pleasant light orange scent creates a fresh atmosphere, while the natural odor counteractants ensure long-lasting freshness without harsh chemicals.

Deep Cleaning for All Surfaces

Whether you're cleaning tile, concrete, walls, or even carpets, Eco Strong's enzyme-based formula works effectively on all surfaces. It also doubles as a safe laundry additive, making it a versatile cleaning solution for pet owners.

Easy to Use and Eco-Friendly

Simply dilute and spray this concentrate on large hard surfaces or use it directly on smaller soft surfaces for spot treatments. No need for multiple products—this one covers it all. Plus, it’s made with non-toxic, natural ingredients and is proudly manufactured in America.

Safe for Pets and People

Eco Strong is not a disinfectant, but its safe, pet-friendly formula ensures no harmful chemicals are left behind. Ideal for frequent use in pet areas, it leaves your space clean, odor-free, and welcoming.
