Mrs. Meyer's Clean Day Pet Stain & Odor Fighter – Orange Blossom Scent, 16oz

Keep your pet’s space fresh and clean with this powerful, natural odor and stain remover. Perfect for tackling those tricky pet messes without harsh chemicals.

When accidents happen, it's essential to have a cleaner that actually works. Our Clean Day Pet Stain & Odor Fighter is formulated with advanced cleaning technology to neutralize odors and target stains on soft surfaces like carpets, rugs, upholstery, bedding, and clothes.

Unlike traditional cleaners that just mask smells, this product uses micro-helpers and essential oils to eliminate pet odors at the source, offering up to 7 days of fresh, clean results. You'll love its Orange Blossom scent—a bright, floral aroma inspired by blooming citrus that feels as refreshing as a sunny orchard.

Committed to sustainability and safety, this product is made with USDA Biobased formulas and comes in a recyclable bottle. It’s also Leaping Bunny certified cruelty-free and free from harsh ingredients like bleach, hydrogen peroxide, parabens, phthalates, and glycol solvents.

Whether you're training a puppy or managing a more mature pet, this stain and odor fighter is a reliable, eco-conscious choice for any pet owner. Make your home smell like a better place—every time.
