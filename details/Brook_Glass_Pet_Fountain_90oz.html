Brook Glass Pet Fountain (90oz/2.7L)

A stylish, safe, and hygienic solution for your pet’s hydration needs.

All Glass Construction – Zero Compromises

BROOK is designed with all glass components for maximum safety and hygiene. Unlike plastic or stainless steel fountains, glass is non-porous, preventing the growth of bacteria and making it easy to clean. Its patent-pending design is not only safe for your pets but also beautiful to look at, blending seamlessly into any home decor.

Safe Hydration for Your Pets

BROOK ensures your pets stay hydrated with crystal-clear, fresh water. The transparent glass allows you to see the water level and quality at a glance, giving you peace of mind. This is the perfect choice for cat owners and pet parents of small dogs who prioritize clean and healthy drinking water.

Easy to Clean & Maintain

Our glass components detach effortlessly for quick hand or dishwasher cleaning. No more awkward scrubbing or complicated parts – just simple, effective maintenance. A cleaning brush is included to make the process even smoother.

Complete Filtration System

BROOK features a dual filtration system that includes a particle filter ring and activated carbon filter. This ensures clean, crisp, and refreshing water for your pets. The first set of filters comes included, and replacement filters are both reliable and affordable.

Designed for Them – Comfort and Style

Adjust the fountainhead to suit your pet’s drinking preference, whether it's a cat or a small dog. Choose from 3 flow intensity levels to keep your pet interested in drinking. A subtle blue LED light gently illuminates the flowing water, encouraging hydration and adding a touch of elegance to your pet’s space. Optional light cover is included for a more discreet look.

BROOK Glass Pet Fountain is more than a water dispenser—it’s a serene, functional oasis for your furry friends. With its quiet operation and modern design, it’s an ideal addition to any pet-friendly home.
