SgurFin Ceramic Cat Water Fountain - 70oz with Multi-Filtration System

Designed to keep your cat or dog hydrated and healthy, this modern ceramic water fountain offers a sleek, functional solution for pet watering needs.

Durable and Easy to Clean Ceramic Construction

Engineered with high-temperature ceramic clay, the SgurFin fountain features a smooth, scratch-resistant surface that resists staining and keeps your pet’s water bowl looking bright. This material is not only long-lasting but also makes cleaning a breeze, helping prevent issues like black chin and ensuring fresh, clean water for your furry friends.

70 oz Large Capacity for All Felines

The 70-ounce capacity is ideal for small to medium-sized cats, providing enough water without needing frequent refills. This is a great choice for busy pet owners who want to ensure their pets stay hydrated all day, every day.

Multi-Filtration System for Cleaner Water

Equipped with an activated carbon filter and pre-sponge filter, this fountain efficiently removes sediment, debris, and pet hair from the water. It ensures a constant flow of fresh, clean, and purified water for your pet. For optimal performance, we recommend replacing the filter every 4 weeks.

Ultra-Quiet Pump with Low Water Consumption

Thanks to our whisper-quiet pump, the SgurFin fountain operates at less than 30dB, so you can enjoy the sound of flowing water without worrying about disturbing your pet or your sleep. It’s energy-efficient and designed to run smoothly, even overnight.

360° Drinking Area for All Angles

Pets naturally prefer flowing water, and the SgurFin fountain delivers just that with its 360° all-round drinking area. This design encourages your cat or dog to drink more, ensuring they stay well-hydrated regardless of where they are positioned around the bowl.

Complete Pet Water Fountain Kit

Each fountain comes with 4 replacement filters and a sponge, ensuring you have everything you need for long-term use. It’s a perfect addition to your pet’s daily care routine and ideal for promoting hydration and well-being at home.

Why Choose SgurFin?

SgurFin pet water fountain combines style, functionality, and innovation to make your pet's drinking experience more enjoyable. With its sleek white ceramic design, efficient filtration system, and ultra-quiet operation, it's the perfect choice for pet owners who value quality and convenience.
