Automatic Parakeet Bird Bath with Pump

A stylish and functional solution for keeping your feathered friends clean and happy

Sturdy and Safe Design

Our bird bath is crafted from durable, non-toxic, and odorless materials, ensuring a safe and hygienic space for your pets. The lightweight construction makes it easy to handle, while the included 3 small chew-resistant balls add an extra layer of protection. It's the perfect size at 6.5*4.72*5.12 inches, ideal for larger birds like parrots, budgies, and lovebirds.

Easy to Clean & Detachable

This easy-to-clean bird shower fountain features a detachable design that allows for simple maintenance. Just wipe and rinse to keep your bird's bathing area spotless, making it a hassle-free addition to your pet's daily routine.

Healthy Bathing Experience

Regular bathing is crucial for maintaining your bird's health and well-being. It helps regulate their body temperature, moisturizes their skin, and keeps them fresh. Our bird bathtub provides a perfect environment for your feathered friends to enjoy a refreshing dip right in their cage.

Powerful & Quiet Operation

Equipped with a USB-powered pump, this bird shower operates ultra-quietly, ensuring a peaceful environment for your pets. The built-in dry-run protection system automatically stops the pump when water levels are low, preventing damage and enhancing safety.

Smart Automatic Water Flow

Designed with a multi-faucet system that connects via USB and a power plug, this bird pool bath is easy to set up. Simply fill it with water, turn on the faucet switch, and enjoy automatic water flow. It's a great way to keep your birds clean and reduce your workload, especially during the warmer months.
