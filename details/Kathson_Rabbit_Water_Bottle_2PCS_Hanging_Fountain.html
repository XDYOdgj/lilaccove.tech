Kathson 2PCS Rabbit Water Bottle - 17oz Hanging Water Fountain for Small Pets

Designed with your busy lifestyle in mind, the Kathson 2PCS Rabbit Water Bottle is the perfect solution for keeping your small pets hydrated when you're not around. Its automatic dispenser ensures a steady water flow, so your bunny, chinchilla, guinea pig, hedgehog, or ferret can drink without hassle, mess, or disruption.

Easy to Use and Customize

This versatile water feeder can be attached to the inside or outside of your pet's cage, depending on your preference. For pets that are more active or curious, placing it outside the cage is ideal. The snap-on design makes it simple to remove for cleaning and refilling with just one hand.

Quiet and Efficient Water Flow

Unlike traditional water bottles with noisy ball bearings, the Kathson water fountain uses a gravity flow system. This delivers smooth, silent water to your pet while also helping to prevent overflow and keep things tidy.

Comfortable and Hygienic Drinking Experience

The hanging design makes it easier for your pet to access water at their preferred height. No more struggling to lift their head for a drink, helping them stay more relaxed and comfortable. It also keeps the water cleaner and reduces the risk of mouth inflammation.

Built to Last with Leak and Dust Protection

Featuring a secure sealing mechanism, the Kathson 2PCS water bottles are both leakproof and dustproof. This ensures your pet gets clean, fresh water while maintaining a safe and healthy environment.
