BNOSDM 91" x 63" Washable Bunny Cage Liner for Rabbits, Chinchillas, Cats, Ferrets, and Dogs

A versatile and durable solution for your pet's comfort and safety with a non-slip design and easy-to-clean surface.

Designed for Comfort

This large rabbit mat is an ideal bedding solution for small animals like rabbits, guinea pigs, ferrets, chinchillas, cats, and dogs. Its soft and breathable material provides a comfortable resting place, helping your pets feel safe and cozy in their habitat. The lining includes folds from packaging, so it's recommended to roll it in the opposite direction and flatten it before use to avoid any potential damage from tearing.

Easy to Clean, Reusable Design

Our washable rabbit cage liner is perfect for pet owners who prioritize hygiene. It can be hand-washed or machine-washed for multiple uses without losing its shape or quality. This makes it a cost-effective and eco-friendly choice for daily use in your pet's home.

Non-Slip Safety Bottom

Equipped with an anti-slip silicone base, this mat stays firmly in place on any surface. Whether it's inside a cage, on the floor, or in a pet crate, it ensures that your pet won't move or slip, providing a stable and secure environment for them.

Multi-Purpose Use

From rabbit cages to dog kennels, this mat is designed to be a functional addition to multiple areas of your home. It doubles as a decorative piece for your living space and can be used under furniture, on balconies, in cars, or even as a tatami mat for extra comfort.

Excellent Customer Service

We're committed to helping you create the best possible environment for your pets. If you have any questions or need assistance, our team is always ready to support you with reliable and friendly service.
