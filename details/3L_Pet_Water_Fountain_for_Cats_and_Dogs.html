APD APETDOLA Cat Water Fountain

A multi-level pet fountain designed to keep your furry friends hydrated and happy all day long.

Multi-Pet Friendly Design

This 3-level cat water fountain with 3 drinking areas is perfect for households with multiple pets. It can comfortably serve up to 4 cats and 2 dogs at the same time. The varying heights are ideal for pets of different sizes, reducing strain on their necks and spines while they drink.

Sustainable 3L Capacity

With a 3L/101oz capacity, this pet fountain ensures a steady water supply for your pets. Its spacious water bowl is built to last and makes for a great drinking station for both felines and canines. Quiet water flow also maintains a peaceful environment, keeping your pets calm and relaxed.

Safe and Convenient Wireless Pump

Unlike traditional models with exposed wires, this cat water fountain features a wireless pump that eliminates the risk of electric shock. The separated base makes installation and cleaning much simpler, allowing for hassle-free maintenance.

Built with Premium Materials

Constructed from BPA-free, food-grade PP resin, this pet water fountain is safe for your animals and designed for long-term use. It includes a detachable container and is dishwasher safe, making cleaning quick and easy. Pet owners will love the time-saving features and durability.

Reliable and Supportive Service

This pet fountain is equipped with a 2.7L/91.3 fl.oz emergency water tank, ensuring continuous hydration even during power outages. Backed by a 12-month warranty, APETDOLA offers quick customer service and 24-hour response for any concerns or issues you may have.

Upgrade your pet's hydration experience with this stylish and functional cat water fountain that combines safety, convenience, and comfort for all your furry companions.
