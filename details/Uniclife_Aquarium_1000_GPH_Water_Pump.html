Uniclife High Performance Aquarium Water Pump

Designed for optimal water circulation, the Uniclife Aquarium 1000 GPH Water Pump is a versatile and powerful solution for both submersible and inline use. This 80W pump features a 10ft high lift and delivers a maximum flow rate of 1000 gallons per hour, making it ideal for large fish tanks, ponds, waterfalls, fountains, sumps and even garden water features.

Versatile Use & Easy Installation

Whether you choose to submerge it fully in the water or use it externally, this pump is ready for your aquarium setup. It comes with inlet and outlet nozzles built-in, which are compatible with water pipes of 1 1/2" and 3/4" inner diameters, ensuring a seamless and secure fit. The included 10-foot waterproof power cord is equipped with a 3-prong grounded plug, providing added safety and peace of mind.

Built to Last & Reliable Performance

Engineered for durability, the Uniclife Water Pump is constructed with a protective ceramic shaft and bearing, offering a long service life of over 25,000 hours. Its robust design allows it to operate continuously for 24 hours a day, making it perfect for environments where constant water movement is essential.

Convenient & Quiet Operation

The pump features a detachable design for easy maintenance and cleaning without the need for tools. Its 4 strong suction cups ensure stability while in use, and the inclusion of a top handle allows for effortless handling and positioning. Additionally, the low-noise operation ensures it won't disturb your aquatic environment or your peace of mind.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced aquarist, this Uniclife Aquarium Water Pump is the perfect choice for maintaining clean, oxygenated and well-circulated water in your aquatic setups. Enhance your tank's health and aesthetics with an efficient, reliable and easy-to-use pump designed for all your water feature needs.
