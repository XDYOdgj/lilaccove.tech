Neobay Cat Door Latch with Stabilizer

Keep your dog out of the litter box while allowing your cat easy access. This adjustable door prop is an economical alternative to expensive pet gates and interior pet doors. Perfect for pet owners who want a simple, effective, and safe solution.

Secure & Adjustable Design

Neobay's cat door latch features a stabilizer and 7 adjustable sizes, making it ideal for cats of all ages and weights. The opening dimensions range from 1.5" to 6", so you can customize the fit to suit your pet's needs. Whether your kitten is small or your cat has grown, this latch adapts with ease.

Safe for Pets and Kids

Our cat door stopper includes a plastic stabilizer at the loop end, ensuring it keeps the door open without closing. This extra feature prevents accidental pinching of tails or fingers, offering peace of mind for both pets and children. It's an ideal solution for keeping your cat comfortable and your home safe.

Easy to Install, No Damage

Install your Neobay cat door latch in seconds! Simply hang one end on the door handle or knob and insert the other into the door frame striker plate. No tools, no cutting, no adhesives or screws needed. This means no paint damage, no door modifications, and a hassle-free setup for your pet's needs.

Portable and Convenient

Designed for on-the-go pet owners, this latch is easy to use with one hand from inside or outside the door. It's compact and lightweight, making it perfect for carrying when traveling or on vacation. Keep it with you and your furry friends wherever you go.

Three Models for Every Need

Neobay offers three types of cat door latches to suit different household requirements. Model B0929NP7CT can keep dogs and children up to 120lbs out using 3M adhesive. Model B0BK9WK3XD provides a no-adhesive installation for immediate use, and Model B0CZ3478HP is built to keep pets over 150lbs out. Choose the one that fits your lifestyle and pet's size.
