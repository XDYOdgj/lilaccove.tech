The AQUANEAT Small Fountain Pump is a versatile and reliable solution for a variety of water-related needs. With an adjustable flow rate and a built-in control knob, you can customize the water flow to suit your aquarium, garden fountain, or hydroponic system. The maximum theoretical flow rate is 80 gallons per hour (300 liters per hour), making it ideal for small to medium-sized setups.

Designed for easy installation and use, this submersible water pump can be submerged in water for optimal performance. It's recommended to keep the maximum lift height at 1 foot for best efficiency. Keep in mind that flow rate decreases as lift height increases or with greater pipe resistance. For instance, at 0 feet (without a water pipe), it delivers 80 GPH, while at 1.5 feet, the output reduces to around 25 GPH.

Featuring two optional outlets, the pump is compatible with tubing of 5/16" or 1/2" inside diameter. Its compact size measures 2" x 1.75" x 1.25" (length x width x height), allowing you to easily hide or disguise it in your setup. The included 6-foot power cord provides convenient placement and reliable power throughout your water feature.

Whether you're maintaining an aquarium, setting up a decorative water fountain, or running a hydroponic or irrigation system, this pump is a great addition. It's perfect for creating soothing water features in your home or garden and supports a range of applications, including table fountains, waterfalls, and pond circulation.

Important Notes for Optimal Performance:
- Submersion Requirement: Ensure the pump is fully submerged in water to avoid overheating and damage.
- Inlet Clearance: Maintain at least 50mm clearance between the water inlet and the water surface.
- Air Removal: When starting up or after water replacement, gently shake the pump to remove any trapped air.
