Catit LED Flower Fountain with Triple Action Filter

Keep your cat hydrated and happy with this stylish, functional water fountain designed for discerning feline drinkers. The Catit LED Flower Fountain offers three different water flow settings, giving your cat the freedom to choose how they drink. With a built-in triple action filter, it ensures fresh, clean water every time, helping reduce the risk of bacteria buildup and keeping the water better tasting for your pet.

Key Features

  • 3 adjustable flow settings to cater to picky drinkers
  • LED light adds a calming effect to the fountain
  • Triple action filter for optimal water purification
  • Energy-efficient pump with a power cord for reliable performance
  • Large capacity of up to 3 liters for continuous use
  • BPA-free materials for safe and healthy pet use
  • Compact design (9.0 x 8.3 x 7.7 inches) that saves space
  • Perfect complement to the Catit Multi Feeder for a complete feeding station
  • Easy to assemble and maintain

Why Choose This Fountain?

Your cat deserves the best when it comes to hydration. This fountain not only provides fresh, oxygenated water but also helps promote healthier drinking habits. The LED light adds a soothing atmosphere, while the ergonomic design ensures seamless integration with other Catit products. It's an excellent investment for any cat owner looking to upgrade their pet's water station with style and function.

Important Notes

For best performance, maintain proper water levels. If the pump is making unusual noise, it may be due to low water levels. Always ensure the connection is watertight and dustproof when using an extension cord. With its modern design and efficient performance, the Catit LED Flower Fountain with Triple Action Filter is an ideal addition to your cat's daily routine.
