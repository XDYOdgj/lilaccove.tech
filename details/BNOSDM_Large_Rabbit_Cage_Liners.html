BNOSDM 79x55In Large Rabbit Cage Liners

Elevate your pet's comfort with these premium washable bunny mats, designed for long-lasting use and easy maintenance. Perfect for rabbits, guinea pigs, ferrets, and even larger pets like dogs, these floor protect rugs offer a safe, soft surface to rest and play.

Soft & Cozy Surface for Pets

Constructed from high-quality polyester, these rabbit pads provide a gentle, warm spot for your furry friends to relax. The plush top layer ensures comfort, while the reinforced bottom offers durability and seamless integration into pet enclosures.

Non-Slip Technology for Stability

Equipped with a textured non-woven fabric bottom, these mats stay firmly in place, preventing slippage during play or rest. Ideal for use under pet crates or in playpen setups, this feature ensures your pet can move freely with confidence and safety.

Versatile & Reusable Design

These multifunctional rabbit pads double as bedding for cages, kennels, and litter boxes. Easy to clean and long-lasting, they are perfect for indoor and outdoor use, as well as travel. Simply vacuum, hand or machine wash, and dry at low temperature for optimal performance.

Perfect Fit for Every Home Enclosure

Available in three sizes: 63x47 inches, 79x55 inches, and 91x63 inches, these rabbit fence rugs are suitable for pets of all breeds and life stages. Measure your existing enclosure to ensure the best fit and maximum comfort.

Important Care Instructions

For longevity, air dry or use low temperature drying after washing. These mats are not leak-proof, so it's recommended to use them with disposable pee pads for added protection and mess-free experience.

Whether you're caring for a small animal or a playful dog, these rabbit cage liners are a must-have for any pet owner looking for a safe, stylish, and functional solution for pet care.
