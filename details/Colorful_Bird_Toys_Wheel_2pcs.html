Colorful Bird Toys Wheel Set for Parakeets and Conures

Introducing our 2-piece bird toy wheel designed to bring joy and stimulation to your feathered friends. The set includes a vibrant yellow flywheel and a charming pink spiral button flywheel, both made to be securely attached to any part of your bird's cage. These toys are ideal for parakeets, conures, African grey parrots, and many other bird species.

When your bird rotates the toy, the built-in ball inside makes a delightful sound, keeping your pet entertained and excited. This interactive feature helps to alleviate boredom and promote a happy, active environment for your bird to explore and play in.

Crafted from a durable and hard composite material, these toys provide a great outlet for chewing, supporting the natural behavior of birds. This helps to keep their beaks healthy and prevents unnecessary overgrowth, making them perfect for long-term use.

With a diameter of 3.15 inches, these toys are designed to be safe and suitable for a wide range of bird sizes. Whether you have a small parakeet or a larger African grey parrot, our wheels are built for comfort and engagement.
