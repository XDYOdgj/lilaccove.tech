SongLong Submersible Pump – Quiet, Efficient & Durable

Enhance your aquatic setup with our ultra-quiet submersible pump designed for long-lasting performance. Ideal for aquariums, ponds, fountains, and more, this 400GPH pump delivers powerful water flow while keeping your environment peaceful and clean.

Ultra-Quiet, High Performance

Experience the difference with a pump that operates so quietly you might not even hear it. Built with high-quality materials, including stainless steel components, it ensures durability and efficient water movement without disrupting your relaxation or your fish's habitat.

Adjustable Flow & High-Lift Design

With an adjustable flow control knob, you can customize the water pressure to suit your needs. This pump provides a maximum flow of 400 gallons per hour and a steady high lift of up to 6.5 feet, making it perfect for fountains, hydroponics, and aquariums that require consistent water circulation.

Secure Installation & Long Cord

Featuring four strong suction cups, the pump stays firmly in place on your aquarium or pond. The 6-foot power cord offers flexibility for easy setup and placement, regardless of whether you install it vertically or horizontally.

3 Nozzles for Custom Flow

Equipped with three interchangeable nozzles, this pump allows you to adjust water flow for different applications. The threaded fittings ensure stability and easy connection, while the detachable design makes cleaning effortless and hassle-free.

Detachable & Easy to Clean

Our pump is designed for easy maintenance. The detachable parts can be removed without the need for tools, making cleaning simple and efficient. Whether you're maintaining your fish tank or managing a hydroponic system, we've got you covered.

Choose the SongLong Submersible Pump for reliable, quiet operation and effortless maintenance. Whether it’s for your home aquarium, a decorative fountain, or a hydroponic garden, this pump is built to last and perform.
