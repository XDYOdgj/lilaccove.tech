Weruva Cats in the Kitchen - Pantry Party Pouch Variety Pack (3oz, Pack of 12)

Treat your feline friend to a delightful mix of flavors with our Pantry Party Pouch Variety Pack. Packed with premium, all-natural proteins like cage-free chicken, wild salmon, and grass-fed beef, each pouch is infused with a rich, savory gravy that cats simply love.

This variety pack includes a balanced mix of 2 each of: Pumpkin Lickin' Chicken, Chick Magnet, Love Me Tender, and Pumpkin Jack Splash, along with 1 of By Land and 2 of By Sea. Completing the pack is Mack, Jack & Sam for a well-rounded taste adventure every day.

Designed for discerning pet owners, Cats in the Kitchen by Weruva offers unique, wholesome recipes crafted with care. These meals are perfect for your kitchen carnivore, providing essential nutrients and the satisfaction of a truly meat-based diet.

Our cat food is made without grains, gluten, starch, or carrageenan, and it's free from corn, wheat, soy, artificial colors, and preservatives. This clean, natural formula is ideal for pets with dietary sensitivities or those seeking the best in quality.

Supporting a healthy skin and coat, this wet cat food is enriched with sustainably-sourced seafood and fish oil, packed with Omega-3 and Omega-6 fatty acids. Help your cat shine with every meal.
