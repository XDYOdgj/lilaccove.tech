Gouccpu 4.92 Inch 12 LEDs Fish Tank Light

Create a dreamlike underwater atmosphere for your aquarium with this elegant and functional bubble light. Designed to enhance both the visual appeal and well-being of your aquatic pets.

Beautiful Lighting for a Serene Aquarium

This 4.92 inch round plastic bubble disc features 12 colorful LED lights that create a soft, glowing gradient. Ideal for lighting your tank and making it a captivating focal point in your home.

Efficient Oxygenation Without an Air Pump

Perfectly designed to oxygenate your tank naturally, this bubble lamp produces small, rhythmic bubbles without the need for an air pump. Simply connect it to an air tube and attach the tube to the pump of your choice.

Easy to Install & Reusable Design

Submerge this buoyant lamp into the water, and let the gentle bubbling create a soothing and romantic environment. When the light dims or bubbles decrease, just clean the disc and use it again for long-lasting enjoyment.

Recommended for Night Use

For a peaceful night in, use this bubble lamp with a 2.5w or higher air pump. It provides natural oxygenation and soft lighting without the need for additional equipment, helping you relax while watching your fish swim quietly.

A Perfect Addition to Your Aquarium Decor

Whether you're a seasoned aquarist or a new hobbyist, this Gouccpu underwater bubble lamp is an ideal way to enhance your tank's ambiance, provide gentle oxygenation, and enjoy the beauty of your aquatic companions.
