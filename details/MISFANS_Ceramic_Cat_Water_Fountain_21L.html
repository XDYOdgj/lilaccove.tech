MISFANS Ceramic Cat Water Fountain - 2.1L/71oz

A stylish and healthy solution for your feline's hydration needs.

Why Choose the MISFANS Ceramic Cat Water Fountain?

Designed with premium ceramic material, this cat water fountain is BPA-free and free of plastics, ensuring a safe and healthy drinking experience for your pets. The smooth ceramic surface is not only easy to clean but also prevents the buildup of impurities, making it more hygienic than plastic alternatives.

Easy Setup and Maintenance

With a built-in pump and detachable parts, setting up and cleaning this cat fountain has never been simpler. It’s perfect for busy pet owners who want to keep their indoor cats well-hydrated without the hassle of complicated assembly or maintenance.

Advanced Filtration for Cleaner Water

Equipped with a dual-layer filtration system, this cat water fountain ensures clean, fresh water for your pet. The sponge filter removes hair and debris, while the activated carbon filter eliminates odors and impurities, enhancing the taste of water for your cat.

Ultra-Quiet Operation for Peaceful Living

Operating at just under 20dB, the ultra-quiet pump keeps your home quiet and comfortable. With only 0.7W power, it is energy-efficient and ideal for long-term use. This cat fountain is designed to blend seamlessly into your living space, allowing both pets and owners to enjoy peace of mind.

Encourage Hydration with Natural Drinking Modes

The MISFANS cat fountain offers two modes—free-flowing tap and bubbling stream—to mimic natural water sources. This encourages cats to drink more, supporting better hydration and reducing the risk of urinary or kidney issues. The elegant copper spout also ensures a better taste of water, free from metallic residue.

Perfect for Indoor Cats

This high-capacity cat water fountain is ideal for indoor cat owners looking for a reliable, easy-to-maintain, and stylish drinking solution. The all-ceramic body and copper spout offer durability and aesthetic appeal while ensuring optimal water quality for your pet's health.
