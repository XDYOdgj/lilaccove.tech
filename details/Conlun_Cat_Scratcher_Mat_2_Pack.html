This cat scratch pad is made from 100% natural sisal fiber, known for its strength and resilience. It can withstand even the sharpest claws, making it a long-lasting solution for your feline friend. Secure & Easy to Use

Equipped with an anti-slip latex backing, this wall-mounted cat scratcher stays firmly in place wherever you put it. Let your cat channel their energy and scratching urges in a designated space. This mat is ideal for protecting your couches, carpets, and other furniture from scratches. It's a great way to keep your pets entertained while preserving your home decor.

Versatile & Affordable

This versatile mat can be used as a cat scratcher, litter mat, play mat, or even a corner scratcher. Its portable design fits perfectly in any room, from living areas to bedrooms. Perfect Gift for Cats
