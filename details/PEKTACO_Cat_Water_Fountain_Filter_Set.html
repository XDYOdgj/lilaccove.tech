PEKTACO Cat Water Fountain Filters & Pre-Filter Sponges

Keep your pet's water clean and fresh with our high-quality replacement filters and pre-filter sponges. Perfect for PEKTACO's 108oz/3.2L and 84oz/2.5L pet water fountains.

Compatibility & Package Includes

Our filters are specifically crafted to work with PEKTACO's 108oz and 84oz pet fountains, ensuring a seamless fit and optimal performance. Each package comes with 3 replacement filters and 3 pre-filter sponges, making it easy to maintain your pet's water system.

Advanced Filtration System

Featuring a multi-stage filtration process, our system includes a pre-sponge filter, activated carbon, and ion exchange resin. This combination effectively removes hair, heavy metals, and dirt, helping keep your pet's water clean and odor-free throughout the day.

Enhance Your Pet's Health

These filters are designed to promote healthy hydration by keeping your pet's water fresh and clean. With regular use, your pet is more likely to drink consistently, which can help reduce the risk of urinary tract infections and support overall wellbeing.

Long-Lasting & Reliable

The pre-filter sponges around the pump help extend its lifespan by trapping large particles and debris before they reach the main filtration system. Combined with durable replacement filters, this set ensures cleaner water, longer pump life, and greater efficiency for your pet's fountain.

Peace of Mind with Support

Enjoy worry-free pet care with our 24-hour customer service support. Whether you need help with installation, replacement, or have any other questions, our team is ready to assist you promptly and efficiently.
