Designed with your cat's health and comfort in mind, the MISFANS Cat Water Fountain offers a stylish and efficient solution for keeping your feline friend hydrated. Advanced Filtration System

Featuring an advanced dual-chamber filter, this fountain ensures your cat drinks only the cleanest, freshest water. The precise holes in the filtration chamber help trap debris and pet hair as water flows, while the ion exchange resin softens the water. The upgraded pump system is designed for simplicity and durability. It can be easily installed and disassembled without tools, reducing the risk of clogs. Cats are naturally sensitive to blue light, which is why the MISFANS fountain includes a built-in blue LED light. This light helps your cat locate the fountain in the dark, encouraging regular water consumption. It also assists pet owners in monitoring the water level easily and refilling when necessary.
