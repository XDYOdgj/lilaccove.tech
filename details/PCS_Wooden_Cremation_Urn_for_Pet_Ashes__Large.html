PCS Cremation Urns for Pet Ashes - Wooden Decorative Urn for Dog and Cat

Honour your beloved pet with a beautifully crafted wooden urn designed for a lasting tribute. This elegant cremation urn is handcrafted, durable, and built with care to preserve your pet's memory.

High Quality & Durable Construction

Our pet memorial urns are made from solid wood, ensuring both strength and a timeless appearance. Each urn is designed to be a lasting keepsake, providing a dignified resting place for your pet. The sliding door on the bottom is secure yet easy to open when needed, making it convenient for families to handle the process with grace.

Perfect Size for All Pet Sizes

Choose from three sizes to accommodate your pet's needs. The Large size urn is ideal for holding the ashes of a larger pet or adult pet up to 180 lbs. It measures 8.5 x 6.5 x 4.5 inches and has a generous capacity of 210 cubic inches, ensuring ample space for your pet's remains.

Exquisite Craftsmanship & Design

Each urn is created in a professional urn factory with modern tools and skilled artisans. The attention to detail and craftsmanship makes this decorative urn a meaningful addition to any home, capable of standing as a beautiful symbol of love and loss.

Customer Satisfaction & Quality Assurance

At PCS, we are committed to providing the highest quality and customer satisfaction. Our urns are designed to meet and exceed your family’s expectations, offering a reliable and emotionally significant solution for your pet's memorial.

More Than a Simple Urn

This cremation keepsake urn is not just a container — it's a heartfelt tribute to your cherished pet. Whether placed in a home, garden, or memorial service, it serves as a sacred and beautiful reminder of the bond you shared. A perfect way to remember your beloved furry friend in style.
