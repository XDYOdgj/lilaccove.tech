SLSON Reptile Plants Hanging Silk Terrarium Decor

Bring a peaceful touch of greenery to your pet's enclosure with these stunning hanging silk plants. Designed to enhance the natural habitat of reptiles, amphibians, and pets, they add life and aesthetics to your setup.

Create an Authentic Environment

These plastic terraria plants are crafted to mimic the lush surroundings of your pet’s natural habitat. Featuring soft, realistic silk leaves, they offer a lifelike look that brings color and character to your aquarium or reptile tank. Perfect for bearded dragons, lizards, geckos, snakes, and hermit crabs, these plants make your pet feel right at home in a natural setting.

Built for Safety and Durability

Made from non-toxic polyethylene plastic, these terrarium plants are safe for your beloved pets and designed to last. Unlike real plants, they don’t need water or sunlight, making them ideal for indoor enclosures where natural conditions are hard to maintain. The soft texture of the leaves also ensures your pet stays comfortable and unharmed.

Simple and Quick Installation

The built-in suction cups make it easy to attach these decorative plants to the glass of your tank, whether it's a hermit crab habitat, a fish tank, or a reptile enclosure. No tools are required, and the lightweight design makes setup hassle-free for any pet owner.

Get the Best Look on First Use

For the most realistic display, start by rinsing the plant in warm water and soaking it for 15-30 minutes. This helps relax the fabric and gives it a lush, natural appearance. Once ready, simply hang it on your tank using the suction cup for a secure and beautiful setup.

What’s in the Box?

Each package includes 1x 12-inch hanging silk terrarium plant with a suction cup. Ideal for home decor, reptile habitat decoration, or as a calming addition to your pet's environment. A perfect choice for anyone looking to enhance the look and feel of their pet's living space.
