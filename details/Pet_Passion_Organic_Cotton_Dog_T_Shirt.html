Pet Passion - Multipack 100% Organic Cotton Dog Short Sleeve T-Shirt

Designed for comfort and style, our dog tees are perfect for all breeds and all seasons. The perfect blend of softness and durability makes them ideal for daily walks, playtime, or cozy indoor moments.

Why Choose This Organic Cotton T-Shirt?

  • Sustainable & Eco-Friendly: Made from 100% organic cotton, certified by GOTS, and printed on recycled paper. Crafted in a solar-powered facility that cares for the planet as much as your pup.
  • Ultimate Comfort: Soft, breathable fabric ensures your dog stays cool and relaxed in warm weather, and cozy in cooler climates. Ideal for all-day wear without irritation or discomfort.
  • Functional Design: Featuring a front leash opening, these shirts make it easy to attach harnesses or collars without hassle. Perfect for active dogs who need a little extra freedom.
  • Perfect Fit: With a relaxed neck and sleeve opening, these tees are easy to put on and take off, giving your dog full range of motion for all activities. Choose from a wide range of sizes to fit your furry friend just right.
