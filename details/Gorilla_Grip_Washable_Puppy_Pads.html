Gorilla Grip Washable Puppy Pads

Durable and Reusable Pet Pad for Training Puppies, Dog Crates, and Comfortable Living Spaces

Designed with a strong 4-layer structure, these washable puppy pads are built to last. The soft quilted top layer is resilient and tear-resistant, providing a cozy surface for your pet. Beneath that, two leak-proof layers efficiently absorb and spread moisture, keeping the area below dry and mess-free. The non-adhesive slip-resistant backing features thousands of silicone grip dots that securely anchor the pad in place, preventing it from sliding or spreading messes.

With a 100% waterproof design, these durable dog crate mats are perfect for protecting your floors, furniture, and sofa from accidents during puppy training or incontinence. They’re also ideal for use in playpens, dog beds, and crates, offering a reliable solution for all your pets’ needs.

These washable puppy pads have been third-party tested for safety and quality, and they can be washed up to 300 times. That means you save money and reduce waste by choosing a reusable alternative to single-use pads. The pads have received the OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification, ensuring they're free from harmful substances and safe for your family and pets.

Whether you're training a new puppy or need a dependable incontinence pad for your senior dog, Gorilla Grip provides an easy, effective, and versatile solution. The sizing fits most standard dog crates and can be used as an extra layer of protection in beds, sofas, and other surfaces, offering peace of mind for pet owners everywhere.
