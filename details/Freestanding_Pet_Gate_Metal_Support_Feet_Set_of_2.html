Freestanding Pet Gate Metal Support Feet - Triangle Set of 2 for Extra Wide Foldable Pet Gate

Enhance the stability and safety of your freestanding pet gate with this durable metal support foot set. Crafted from high-quality metal, these feet feature a sleek black satin finish that ensures long-lasting use and complements the PUPETPO pet gate perfectly, adding an elegant touch to your home. Designed for extra balance, the sturdy triangular support feet keep your gate from tipping, allowing it to remain stable even when fully extended to its maximum width. This added security is essential for keeping your pets safe and your home organized. The included anti-slip rubber pads at the bottom protect your floors from scratches and prevent the gate from shifting. They also increase friction between the feet and the gate frame for a secure fit. Installation is effortless with the pre-designed grooves that fit seamlessly into your dog gate. These support feet slide easily into the base of folding pet gates and can be quickly removed when not in use, offering convenience without compromising safety. Dimensions: 11.8" L x 0.8" W x 2.4" H. Snap joint: 0.71" W x 2.3" H. Be sure to measure your door panel thickness and bottom rail height before purchasing. These support feet are specifically designed to work with door panels that are 0.71" thick.
