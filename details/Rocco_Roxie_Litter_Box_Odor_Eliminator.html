Rocco & Roxie Litter Box Odor Eliminator

Keep your cat's litter box smelling fresh and clean with our natural, plant-based odor eliminator. It's designed to absorb even the strongest smells, making a big difference in your home's air quality and your pet's comfort.

Our proprietary blend of essential oils works at a molecular level to neutralize unpleasant odors, leaving your litter box fresh and clean for longer. Say goodbye to lingering smells and hello to a more pleasant living environment for you and your feline friend.

Longer Litter Life

With its moisture-wicking properties, this deodorizer helps extend the life of your litter. It mixes easily with all types of litter, reducing the need for frequent cleaning and saving you money on replacement litter.
