Kathson Parrot Bathtub Bird Automatic Bath with Fountain

Give your feathered friends a refreshing and hygienic bath with this innovative bird bath designed for parrots and other small to medium-sized birds.

This automatic bird bath features a built-in faucet that can be easily activated with a switch. All you need to do is insert 2 AA batteries (not included) and enjoy the gentle flow of water, helping your parrots stay clean and refreshed.

Crafted from high-quality, non-toxic ABS plastic, this bird bath is lightweight, durable, and safe for daily use. Its smooth surface ensures comfort and prevents injuries to your birds.

Regular bathing is essential for maintaining your birds' health. This bird bath helps them remove dirt, dust, and parasites from their feathers, while also providing a cooling effect on hot days and keeping their skin moisturized.

Enhance your bird's daily routine with this fun and interactive bath enclosure. It comes with a variety of small toy attachments that make the experience more enjoyable and encourage healthy bathing habits.

The dimensions of this bird tub are 13.66" x 9.45" x 3.23", making it ideal for long-tailed parrots, budgies, cockatiels, and lovebirds. It's also a great addition to your home decor or a safe children's toy for supervised play.

Designing for ease of use, this bird bath is simple to install and clean. It's a must-have for bird owners who want to provide a spa-like experience for their pets.
