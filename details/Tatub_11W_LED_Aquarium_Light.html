Tatub 11W LED Aquarium Light

Enhance your aquatic environment with the Tatub 11W LED aquarium light designed specifically for 18" to 24" fish tanks. This full-spectrum aquarium light offers a balanced blend of white, blue, red, and green LEDs to support both vibrant fish coloration and healthy plant growth.

10-Level Brightness Control

With 10 adjustable brightness levels, you can customize the light to match the specific needs of your fish or aquatic plants. This ensures optimal conditions, reducing overexposure and promoting thriving plant growth.

Three Lighting Modes

Choose from three lighting modes: white + green + red, blue, or all four colors. Each mode is tailored to different stages of plant development and fish activity, helping you create the perfect underwater atmosphere.

Timer & Memory Function

Enjoy the convenience of a built-in timer with options for 6, 10, or 12 hours. Plus, the memory function remembers your last brightness setting, so your aquarium light turns on automatically at the same brightness every time.

Waterproof & Durable Build

Featuring an IP68 waterproof rating and a reinforced aluminum shell, this light is built for long-lasting use in aquatic environments. It ensures safety and efficient heat dissipation for optimal performance.

Adjustable Bracket Included

The telescoping bracket adjusts from 15.5" to 26.3", making it compatible with most 18" to 24" aquariums. Easy to install and set up, it's a perfect fit for your tank's size and shape.

Ideal for Fish & Plants

Powered by ultra-bright 6500-7000K white LEDs and rich red, blue, and green light, this aquarium light supports both the natural behavior of fish and the growth of aquatic plants. Watch your tank come to life with stunning visuals and vibrant colors.

The Tatub Aquarium Light is a must-have for every aquarium enthusiast looking to create a beautiful, healthy, and well-lit underwater world.
