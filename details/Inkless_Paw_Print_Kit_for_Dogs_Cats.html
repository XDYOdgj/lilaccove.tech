DeerZone Inkless Paw Print Kit

Capture the unique essence of your furry family members with DeerZone's Inkless Paw Print Kit. Designed for pet lovers, this set includes a clean touch ink pad and imprint cards, offering a no-mess, worry-free way to create lasting memorials for dogs and cats with ease.

Featuring two ink pad sizes, this kit caters to a variety of pets. The larger pad measures 3.2" by 5", with a 2.3" by 3.7" ink window, ideal for bigger paws, while the smaller size is perfect for kittens or small dogs. This versatility makes it easy to find the right fit for your pet's footprints.

The process is simple and safe. Simply place the ink pad over the cardstock, gently press your pet's paw onto the pad, and after a few seconds, you'll have a clear, crisp imprint. The inkless design ensures that your pet's paws never come into direct contact with the ink, making it safe and easy for every family.
