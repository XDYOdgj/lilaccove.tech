Aquatrone 550GPH Submersible Pump - Ultra Quiet Fountain Water Pump for Aquariums, Ponds, Hydroponics & More

Enhance your aquatic setup with the Aquatrone 550GPH Submersible Pump, designed for quiet operation, durability, and versatility for all your water-related needs.

Reliable Performance at Its Best

This submersible pump delivers a powerful flow rate of 550 gallons per hour (2000 liters per hour) with a 7.2-foot vertical lift, making it ideal for maintaining clean and oxygenated water in your aquariums, ponds, or hydroponic systems. Powered by a low-energy 30W motor, it ensures efficient water movement without draining your electricity. The corrosion-resistant stainless steel impeller shaft adds to its long-lasting nature, protecting it from damage due to water exposure.

Ultra Quiet & Compact Design

Enjoy the peaceful ambiance of your aquatic setup with the quiet operation of the Aquatrone pump. It uses advanced sound-absorbing suction cups and a low-noise motor to minimize vibrations and noise, making it perfect for indoor environments. Its compact size allows you to easily hide the pump, keeping it out of sight while still being effective. This compact design also makes it easier to install and manage without taking up too much space.

Adjustable Flow for Customized Control

With a built-in flow control valve, you can easily adjust the water flow to suit your specific needs. This feature gives you full control over the water movement, ensuring it's just right for your fish tank or fountain. The pump is also designed to be easily disassembled for maintenance, making it user-friendly and long-lasting. Its fine filtration system prevents dirt particles larger than 2mm from entering, protecting the pump and increasing its lifespan.

Safe & Durable Operation

Designed with safety in mind, this submersible water pump features a grounded system and is equipped with a 6.56-foot heavy-duty weatherproof power cord. This eliminates the risk of stray voltage and ensures safe use for both you and your aquatic life. It also includes an automatic overheating protection system, which switches the pump off if the water level is too low. The use of silicone-based lubricant makes it safe for fish and other aquatic animals instead of traditional toxic oils, making it a trusted choice for all water-based applications.

Multi-Use & Long Warranty

The Aquatrone 550GPH Submersible Pump is designed for a wide range of uses, such as circulating and aerating water in ponds, creating flowing effects in fountains, and even in hydroponic and statuary systems. It comes with three interchangeable nozzles to fit hose sizes like 0.5", 0.62", and 0.75", along with a threaded nozzle design that prevents leaks and ensures consistent performance. Enjoy worry-free use with a 3-year warranty, making this a dependable choice for your water features or aquatic environment.
