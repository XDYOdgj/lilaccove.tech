Treela 2 Pcs Cat Carpet Protector Plastic Runners

Keep your floors clean and safe with Treela's durable cat carpet protectors. Designed to prevent pets from scratching and tearing, these plastic runners offer a reliable, easy-to-install solution for your home.

Protect Your Carpet from Damage

Treela's 2-piece cat carpet protector is the perfect way to shield your floors from unintentional damage caused by your furry friends. Whether your cat is scratching at doorways or leaving stains behind, these runners create a barrier that keeps your carpet in perfect condition for longer.

Sturdy and Durable Material

Made from high-quality PVC, these protectors are strong, firm, and built to last. They resist fraying and breaking, ensuring long-term use without the need for frequent replacements. This material is also non-toxic and safe for use around pets.

Custom Fit for Your Space

Each protector measures approximately 12 x 63 inches (30 x 160 cm), and comes with a thickness of 1.6 mm, making it easy to cut to fit your doorway size. Before ordering, be sure to measure the width of your door and the height from the floor to ensure a perfect fit.

Clear and Non-Slip Design

These transparent plastic runners are easy to spot and clean. They feature reinforced rivets on the bottom for a secure, non-slip grip, ensuring they stay in place even with frequent use. The smooth top surface is gentle on your cat's paws and won't cause discomfort.

Simple and Hygienic Cleaning

Cleaning your cat carpet protector has never been easier. Simply wipe it down with a damp cloth to remove dirt and stains. This hassle-free maintenance keeps your home fresh and your pets happy while saving you time and effort.

Perfect for Every Home

Whether you have a living room, hallway, or entryway, Treela's cat carpet protectors offer a versatile and effective way to maintain a clean, safe environment. Ideal for pet owners looking for practical solutions that balance comfort and protection.

Dimensions: 12 x 63 inch / 30 x 160 cm (width x length)
