Rocco & Roxie Supply Co. Stain & Odor Eliminator

A powerful, safe solution for eliminating tough pet stains and odors from all surfaces in your home.

Safe for Your Family and Pets

This enzymatic stain and odor eliminator is certified safe for use around children and pets. It's free from harsh chemicals, chlorine, and color-damaging agents. With no residue or harmful propellants, it's gentle enough for all carpet types and has earned the Seal of Approval from the Carpet and Rug Institute (CRI).

Tackles Organic Stains and Odors

Our formula goes beyond surface-level cleaning. It targets the source of pet urine, feces, vomit, and other organic spills using powerful enzymes. These enzymes break down and neutralize stubborn stains and strong odors, leaving your home fresh and clean without lingering smells.

Versatile for Every Surface

Whether it's your carpets, floors, furniture, or even pet bedding, this spray works effectively on all types of surfaces. It's designed for use in areas where pets spend their time, making it ideal for dog and cat litter boxes, carriers, and more.

Enzyme-Powered Cleaning

Unlike traditional cleaners that only hide odors, this product uses natural enzymes that actively break down and eliminate the problem. These enzymes work by feeding on ammonia and organic matter, ensuring a deep clean and long-lasting freshness.
