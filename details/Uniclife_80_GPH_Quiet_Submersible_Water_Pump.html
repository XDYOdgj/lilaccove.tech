Uniclife 80 GPH Submersible Water Pump

The perfect solution for maintaining clean and flowing water in your aquarium or fountain.

Versatile Design

This compact submersible water pump is designed for both indoor and outdoor use. Whether you're keeping fish in an aquarium, maintaining a fountain, or even using it for bird baths and sculpture cleaning, its durable build ensures long-term performance in various environments.

Adjustable Flow Control

With a maximum flow rate of 80 gallons per hour and a lift height of 2.4 feet, this pump offers precise control. The rotatable knob lets you easily adjust the flow to suit your specific needs, making it flexible for different setups and water features.

Built for Longevity

Engineered for durability, this pump features a ceramic impeller shaft and epoxy resin casing that protect against wear and corrosion. It can operate for over 20,000 hours, making it ideal for continuous use without frequent replacements.

Easy to Maintain

This pump is designed for hassle-free maintenance. It comes with 3 strong suction cups that secure it firmly in place and help reduce operational noise. The detachable design allows you to remove and clean the unit without tools, ensuring it stays in excellent condition for years.

Complete with Accessories

Every pump is equipped with everything you need to get started. This includes a 5.5-foot UL listed waterproof power cord with a US plug, an adjustable flow regulator, and 2 nozzles compatible with 1/2 inch and 1/3 inch diameter hoses. All components are designed for safe and efficient use in your aquatic setup.

Uniclife makes water flow easy, quiet, and reliable for all your aquatic needs.
